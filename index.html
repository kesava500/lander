Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see results?

Most clients notice improvements in their movement and posture within 4-6 weeks of consistent practice, though individual results may vary.

Do I need special equipment?

Most exercises can be done with minimal equipment. We'll discuss any specific needs during your assessment.

Can you help with chronic pain?

While we work with many clients managing chronic pain, we recommend consulting your healthcare provider before starting any new program.

Are virtual sessions available?

Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual coaching sessions to accommodate your schedule and location.
